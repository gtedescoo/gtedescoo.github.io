A slow Sunday table

We’re a small collective of friends, who started asking ourselves when was the last time we truly sat down with strangers, shared a meal, and stayed present until the end of the evening.

1 — The problem

Phones on the table. Conversations cut short. Music playing, but never really listened to.

2 — The turn

The Rooting Table was born from that — a desire to reverse the rhythm.

3 — The night

A long table, shared plates, soft live music and no rush to leave. Community doesn’t happen fast.

Where to find us

We meet every Sunday of June at Parco Sempione, in the heart of Milano. A quiet green pocket inside the city.

You can reach us easily by foot, bike or metro.

Metro / Tram: M1–M2 Cadorna, M5 Domodossola, Tram 1–19 (5–10 min walking from the park). By bike: multiple entrances and bike paths, with racks near main gates. On foot: easy access from Arco della Pace, Triennale or Cadorna.

Reserve your seat

Dinner seats are limited to keep the evening intimate. Reserve in advance and let us know if you're coming with kids or have any dietary needs.
